Conservation-First EV Routing

Chennai EV Route Optimizer Simulator

This MVP shows why EV routing should optimize more than ETA. It combines real Chennai route geometry and elevation with a battery model that prices congestion uncertainty and protected arrival charge.

What is real
Road ETA, route shape, and elevation are cached from Chennai data.
What is new
We add an uncertainty reserve instead of optimizing only mean kWh.
Why it matters
Protected arrival battery is more useful than a nominal range estimate.

Inputs from reality
Road ETA, route geometry, altitude sampling, weather stress, and stop-go exposure enter the score.
Decision output
The route is ranked on expected energy, reliability reserve, risk, and an ETA tradeoff chosen by the user.
How We Calculate
Expected energy
distance x base EV rate x congestion factor x speed factor + climb - regen + cabin load
Reliability reserve
expected energy x (base uncertainty + stop-go share + scenario volatility + driver bias)
Decision score
energy weight x expected use + reserve weight x reserve + ETA penalty + risk penalty
